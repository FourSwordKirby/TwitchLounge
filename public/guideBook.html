The n00b's Guide to the Twitch Lounge

What is Twitch Lounge?

Twitch Lounge is a web application that serves as a virtual space for Twitch stream viewers to interact with one another via gestures, movement, and semi-private messages.

How do I use the Lounge?

First, login with twitch, then go to a lounge. Once in, you can press the arrow keys to move around. The zoom function lets you view things closer or farther. Messages on the left are local, based on who is near you. Customize your avatar via the "User Settings" popup. Have a good time!

How do I get a Lounge for my stream?

Just add in your username to the end of our site. For example, my username is Nutley2, so I can go to https://twitch-lounge.herokuapp.com/nutley2 for my lounge. Every streamer has a lounge regardless if they've signed up, but only streamers who have signed up can customize their lounge.
